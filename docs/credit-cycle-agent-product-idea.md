# Credit Cycle Agent Product Idea

## Context

The idea is to explore whether information from Ray Dalio's explanation of the economic machine can become a useful product around credit cycles, global debt conditions, and economic signals.

The target is not only a dashboard for people. The longer-term bet is an information layer for smart agents that can consume economic context through subscriptions, APIs, MCP, or another machine-readable interface.

## Practical Starting Point

Starting with a pure API for agents is strategically interesting, but early. The agent market is still forming, and it is unclear who the buyer is, what data format they need, and what they will pay for.

Starting with pure educational content is also risky because it can become endless explanation without a product.

The strongest validation path is a hybrid:

**Signal + explanation**

Each week, publish a human-readable overview of the credit cycle, while internally keeping a fixed structure that can later become API or MCP output.

Example structure:

```text
Country: US
Credit Cycle Phase: tightening / easing / stress / recovery
Key Signals:
- policy rate
- yield curve
- credit growth
- delinquency/default trend
- bank lending standards
- corporate spreads
- household debt stress
Interpretation:
- what changed
- why it matters
- what could break
Confidence: low/medium/high
Sources: ...
```

This validates several things at once:

- Interest from people in simple explanations of economic cycles.
- Which signals are actually useful.
- Which countries, sectors, and markets matter to the audience.
- Whether the content can become a machine-readable data layer.
- Which repeated user questions can become product features.

## Possible Monetization Path

1. Free content: simple explanations of credit cycles and economic signals.
2. Paid newsletter: a weekly "Global Credit Cycle Brief".
3. Dashboard: a map of countries or sectors by credit-cycle phase.
4. API/MCP: structured economic signals for agents.
5. Agent-ready research layer: agents can ask questions like:
   - Where is credit stress rising?
   - Which markets are entering deleveraging?
   - Which sources support this signal?

## Next Validation Question

Which first format is best for validating the idea in 2-4 weeks?

1. Telegram/newsletter: publish fast and observe the reaction.
2. Mini-dashboard: simple page with a credit-cycle index for 5-10 countries.
3. MCP/API prototype: machine-readable economic signals from the start.
4. Research reports: deeper PDF or Notion reports by country or theme.
